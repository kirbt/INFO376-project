The exploration of outer space continues to fascinate humanity. 
Missions to Mars, the Moon, and beyond are pushing the boundaries 
of our knowledge. Scientists use telescopes like the James Webb 
Space Telescope to peer deep into the universe, searching for 
exoplanets and signs of extraterrestrial life. Private companies 
are also playing an increasingly significant role in space travel 
and satellite technology, paving the way for potential lunar 
colonies and deep-space travel.
